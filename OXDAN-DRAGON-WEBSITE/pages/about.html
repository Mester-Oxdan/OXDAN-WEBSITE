Our Story.

From a single line of code to a digital revolution.

The Beginning

Oxdan Production started in 2023 with a simple mission: to bridge the gap between complex developer tools and intuitive design. What began as a passion project quickly evolved into a comprehensive ecosystem of utilities.

We believed that power tools didn't have to look like they were from the 90s. We wanted to bring the elegance of consumer software to the developer experience.

Innovation & Craft

Our expansion into 3D printing was a natural progression of our desire to create. Just as we craft code, we now craft physical objects with the same attention to detail and quality.

Every product, digital or physical, undergoes rigorous testing and refinement. We don't just ship features; we ship experiences.
